Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Leovine ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our website and services to connect with consultants or businesses.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you fill out a form to hire an expert or join our network. This includes:

  • Identity Data: Name, job title, and company name.
  • Contact Data: Email address and phone number.
  • Project Data: Details about your business challenges, project scope, and budget.
  • Technical Data: Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type and version, time zone setting, operating system, and platform.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Facilitate the matching process between businesses and consultants.
  • Communicate with you about your project or application.
  • Analyze website usage and improve our platform functionality using tools like Google Analytics.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We only share data with prospective consultants or clients with your explicit consent for the purpose of a project engagement.

4. Google Analytics & Cookies

We use Google Analytics to help us understand how our website is used. Google Analytics uses "cookies," which are text files placed on your computer, to collect standard internet log information and visitor behavior information in an anonymous form.

The information generated by the cookie about your use of the website (including your IP address) is transmitted to Google. This information is then used to evaluate visitors' use of the website and to compile statistical reports on website activity for Leovine.

5. Data Security

We have implemented appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way. Access to your personal data is limited to those employees, agents, and partners who have a business need to know.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete the data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at: houseofleovine@gmail.com.
